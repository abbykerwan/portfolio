Circadian Rhythms

and the sleep-wake cycle

Sleep patterns are established by circadian rhythms. These regulate the body’s processes during a 24-hour day because the body is naturally aligned with sunrise and sunset.

Morning light exposure, specifically from the sun, has been proven to increase energy levels, reduce the risk of depression, and maintain healthy internal functions.

GG's Routine

7:00 - Wake Window

Set an alarm for the same time every day
(even weekends!)

7:05 - Sun Exposure

Spend 10-15 minutes outside in natural sunlight

These habits help me feel alert and energized. I always wake up feeling my best and ready for the day!

Oral Health

beyond the brush

Most people brush their teeth twice a day and some floss regularly, but there are fewer who know about the benefits of Ayurvedic oil pulling, a popular practice that improves overall wellness, from the mouth to the gut.

Coconut oil has antimicrobial and moisturizing properties, making it the perfect ritual for gum health, bad breath, and cavity prevention.

GG's Routine

7:20 - Oil Pull

Swish coconut oil in the mouth for 5 minutes, then spit into the trash

7:25 - Brush Teeth

Follow with normal brushing to remove any bacteria the oil has collected

My teeth feel cleaner and look whiter. And I’m cavity free!

Mindfulness

to start the day positively

Taking a few moments each morning to clear stress and establish a positive mindset has been shown to improve mental health, productivity, memory, and physical tension.

Rather than scrolling through social media first thing, journaling and meditation are simple ways to uplevel every morning, and often takes as little as five minutes each.

GG's Routine

7:30 - Journaling

Write your intention for the day, something that inspires you to grow and improve

7:35 - Meditation

Sit in silence for 5-10 minutes, focus on your breathing and embrace the stillness

I feel happier and calmer throughout the day when I reframe my mindset!

Digestion

to support the body and mind

Gut health is the latest social media trend, but it shouldn’t be a passing fad. Drinking hot lemon water before breakfast activates your digestive enzymes to prevent bloating and promote detoxification.

Next, consuming a high protein meal within the first hour after waking has been shown to improve satiety, regulate blood sugar, and increase energy levels.

GG's Routine

7:45 - Hot Lemon Water

Squeeze half of a fresh lemon into hot water and sip with a straw before breakfast

8:00 - High Protein Breakfast

Choose high quality whole foods like eggs, turkey, or greek yogurt for 30 grams of protein

I feel satisfied and energized, and my gut health has never been better!

Movement

for a functional and active life

While working out can lead to weight loss and strength gains, getting one hour of intentional movement each morning can also balance stress and mood.

Morning movement improves sleep patterns, boosts productivity, and regulates hormones. Stacking exercise with your regular routine eases the process of building a habit.

GG's Routine

8:30 - Exercise

Complete 60 minutes of strength training, running, yoga, walking or your favorite sport

Choose an activity that you enjoy and that allows you to celebrate your body's abilities

I feel stronger and happier all around. Getting this done early is a huge win!

See for Yourself

Start with one habit that fits into your current morning. Do you have a hard time waking up? Or are you feeling anxious?

Take what resonates with you, leave the rest, and take care of yourself! You deserve to feel your best every day.
